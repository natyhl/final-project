About the project

  • This project has origin in a topic of personal interest. After being overwhelmed by the noise of social media, I went vegan for several months out of concern for emissions, pollution, and sustainability. I ended up facing some health issues that might have or might not have been connected to my diet changes. That seemed like a big risk, considering that I changed my diet only because of a common “Instagram theme”. That experience left me with two questions: Does going vegan actually matter at a global scale? And if yes, what are the biggest contributors to the pollution? I'm going to answer these questions in this project.
